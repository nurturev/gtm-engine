# GTM Motion Decision Framework

## Product-Led Growth (PLG)
**Choose when:** Product is simple enough for self-serve, ACV under $10K, individual users drive adoption (devs, designers), end user can experience value via free trial/freemium.

**Key metrics:** Time-to-value (best: hours), PQL conversion, free-to-paid rate, viral coefficient, expansion revenue from self-serve.

**Warning:** McKinsey analysis of 107 B2B SaaS shows most PLG adopters do NOT see a boost. Only a select subset outperform. PLG is not a silver bullet.

**Best examples:** Cursor ($0 to $500M ARR in <24 months), Lovable ($100M ARR in 8 months).

## Sales-Led Growth (SLG)
**Choose when:** ACV above $25K-$50K, complex products needing implementation, multi-stakeholder buying committees (11-20 per Gartner), buyers expect customization, procurement/legal involved.

**Key frameworks:** MEDDPICC, SPICED, Command of the Message.

## Product-Led Sales (PLS) — The Hybrid
Uses self-serve users as primary funnel for sales.

**Team structure:** Sales-assist (helps adoption), AEs (receive PQLs), SDRs (inbound + mine PQLs).

**Identifying PQLs:** Usage milestones, advanced feature trials, paywall hits, teammate invitations.

**Rules of engagement:** Self-serve and sales must not overlap or cannibalize.

## Community-Led Growth (CLG)
**Key stats:** 2.1x faster revenue growth, 46% higher LTV, $1 invested returns $6.40.

**Critical distinction:** Community as channel (push updates) vs. community as growth driver (members advocate autonomously).

**2025-2026 trend:** Nano/micro communities with higher engagement over large-platform management.

## Partner-Led Growth
**Key stats:** Partner-sourced deals: 40% higher AOV, 53% more likely to close, 46% faster. 58% of revenue for top performers comes from partners.

**Five motions:** Ecosystem partnerships, channel partnerships, referral programs, affiliates, resellers.

## Decision Matrix

| Factor | PLG | SLG | CLG | Partner-Led |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|-------------|
| ACV | <$10K | >$25K | Any | >$10K |
| Product complexity | Low (self-serve) | High (implementation) | Any | Medium-High |
| Buyer | Individual user | C-suite/committee | Practitioner | Varies |
| Sales cycle | Days-weeks | Months | Organic | Medium |
| Key metric | Time-to-value | Win rate, ACV | Engagement, NPS | Partner pipeline |
| CAC profile | Low | High | Low-medium | Medium (shared) |

## The 2025-2026 Reality
Most successful companies run hybrid models. Segment by ACV and buyer:
- Self-serve for SMB
- Sales-assisted for mid-market
- Field sales for enterprise
- Define clear transition rules based on usage, team size, or feature requests

**Critical metric across all:** LTV:CAC ratio target 3:1+, CAC payback under 12 months. Companies currently spend $2 S&M for every $1 new ARR.
