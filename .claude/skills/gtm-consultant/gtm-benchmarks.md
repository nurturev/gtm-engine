# GTM Metrics & Benchmarks (2025-2026)

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Segment | CAC Range | Sales Cycle |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| SMB | $100-$400 | 1-3 months |
| Mid-Market | $400-$800 | 3-6 months |
| Enterprise | $800-$2,000+ | 6-18 months |

Average B2B SaaS: ~$1,200 (14% increase YoY). New CAC Ratio: $2.00 S&M to acquire $1.00 new ARR. Expansion CAC: $1.00 per $1 ARR (2x more efficient than new).
