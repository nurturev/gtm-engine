# Pricing & Packaging Strategy

## The 2025-2026 Landscape

Hybrid pricing is winning. Seat-based dropped from 21% to 15%. Hybrid surged from 27% to 41%. Credits surged 126% YoY.

## Pricing Models

### Seat-Based
- Predictable revenue, simple to understand, easy to forecast
- Doesn't scale with value, penalizes adoption, creates shelfware
- Best for: Collaboration tools where more users = more value

### Usage-Based
- Aligns with value, low barrier to entry, natural expansion
- Revenue volatility, harder to forecast
- Best for: Infrastructure, API products, AI/ML tools

### Hybrid (Dominant in 2025-2026)
- Predictable base + scalable upside
- More complex to communicate
- 65% of SaaS with generative AI features use this

### Credit-Based
- Flexible, works across product lines
- Can confuse customers, obscures true cost
- Best for: Multi-product platforms, AI features with variable cost

## Value-Based Pricing (The North Star)

Find your value metric — the unit that scales as customers get more value.

Questions to find it:
- What unit does your customer measure success by?
- Does pricing scale as customer gets more value?
- Is the metric transparent and predictable?

## The AI Pricing Challenge

Companies with per-seat pricing for AI: 40% lower gross margins, 2.3x higher churn vs usage/outcome-based. AI scales by tokens and compute, not by user.

**Emerging 2026:** Outcome-based pricing. Not "$ per seat" but "$ per ticket resolved" or "$ per invoice processed."

## Land-and-Expand

**Expansion levers:**
1. Seat-based (invite team members)
2. Usage-based (exceed free tier)
3. Feature gating (adopt premium features)
4. Department expansion (new teams adopt)

Top PLG companies: NRR above 120%. Top quartile: 42-48% of new revenue from existing.

**Key stat:** 27% of AI app spend comes through PLG (4x traditional SaaS at 7%).

## Free Tier Design

**Core tension:** Too little = never activate. Too much = no upgrade reason.

**2026 principles:**
- Time-to-value measured in seconds, not sessions
- "Ungate usage; gate safety" — monetize admin/governance tier, not end-user access
- Find precise boundary: enough value to stay engaged, need premium for maximum benefit

**Benchmarks:**
- Freemium to paid: 5-10%
- Free trial to paid: 15-25%
- PQLs convert 5-6x higher than MQLs

## How to Advise on Pricing

1. Start with value metric — what does the customer pay for outcomes?
2. Analyze competitors — where are they on the seat/usage/hybrid spectrum?
3. Consider gross margins — AI-heavy products need usage/outcome pricing
4. Design for expansion — pricing should reward growth, not penalize it
5. Test with segments — different pricing for SMB vs enterprise is normal
6. Keep it simple enough to explain in one sentence
